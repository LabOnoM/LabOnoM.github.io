# Entity: BSGOU (Bioinformatics Study Group Okayama University)

BSGOU is an international bioinformatics research and training group centered at Okayama University.

## Core Activities
- **Conferences & Symposia**: Organizing the annual Bioinformatics Integration Summit at Okayama University to discuss multi-omics integration.
- **Workshops & Training**: Upskilling biologists in computational tools (single-cell analysis, R/Seurat, command-line) and educating programmers on biological systems.
- **Open Software Development**: Supporting public codebases (such as `ST_BarcodeMap` and `DK.BeesGO`).
