# Entity: GSE284271 (Visium HD Nuclei Segmentation)

GSE284271 contains spatial transcriptomics Visium HD data. The custom binning and segmentation pipeline is documented in `GSE284271/01.scRNAseq10xVisiumHD.html`.

## Methodology
This analysis guide integrates cell-segmentation algorithms with high-throughput spatial transcriptomics:
1. **StarDist Segmentation**: Segmenting high-resolution H&E staining images of mouse intestinal tissue to generate a high-fidelity nuclei mask.
2. **Coordinate Mapping**: Mapping coordinate barcodes of Visium HD bins back to the shape and centroid coordinates of segmented cell nuclei.
3. **UMI Summation**: Gene-wise summation of UMI counts in each custom cell boundary to construct a gene-by-binned barcode cell matrix.
4. **AnnData Export**: Exporting centroids, nuclear boundaries, molecule counts, and expression matrices into Seurat and AnnData-compatible formats for downstream Scanpy clustering.
