# Entity: Zebrafish History

Documents the genomic history, evolutionary lineage, and relevance of zebrafish (*Danio rerio*) in modern biomedical research.

## Key Research Areas
1. **Model Organism Advantages**: Optical transparency of embryos, rapid development, and high genetic conservation with humans ($\approx70\%$ ortholog overlap).
2. **Genomic Evolution**: Highlighting duplication events (teleost-specific genome duplication, TSGD) shaping the zebrafish gene landscape.
3. **Wet-Dry Integration**: Using spatial transcriptomics (Stereo-seq / Visium) to map embryonic development phases.
