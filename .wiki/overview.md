# Overview: BSGOU and AROS

This workspace hosts the official public portal for **BSGOU (Bioinformatics Study Group Okayama University)**. 

## Mission
Our mission is to advance bioinformatics through an inclusive, collaborative, and innovative community. Based in Okayama University with global reach, we integrate wet-lab and dry-lab workflows to solve complex biological questions.

## Project Scope
1. **Bioinformatics Resource Site**: Providing blogs, tutorials, and guidelines on methods (such as qPCR, Stereo-seq, and Visium HD).
2. **Open Science Dissemination**: Hosting notebooks, custom tools, and code repositories (e.g., `ST_BarcodeMap`).
3. **Ecosystem Dashboard**: Interfacing with the AROS (Antigravity Research OS) agent system to coordinate multi-agent scientific operations.
