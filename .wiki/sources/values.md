# Source: Core Values of BSGOU

This page synthesizes the 9 core values documents located in the `sources/` directory.

## Core Values List
1. **Contributions (`sources/Contributions.md`)**:
   - Encourages anyone to contribute to co-building BSGOU via Fork and Pull Request workflows.
   - Contributions count towards the [[member-fetch-pipeline]] Poisson normalized contribution rank.
2. **Education for Empowerment (`sources/Education_for_Empowerment.md`)**:
   - Commits to training all members in bioinformatics, regardless of initial skill level.
3. **Global Inclusivity (`sources/Global_Inclusivity.md`)**:
   - Unites a diverse international community bridging boundaries across nationalities and disciplines.
4. **Open Innovation (`sources/Open_Innovation.md`)**:
   - Promotes open-source and open-science methods to ensure reproducibility.
5. **Scientific Integrity and Responsibility (`sources/Scientific_Integrity_and_Responsibility.md`)**:
   - Commits to rigorous data collection, clear validation, and ethical conduct.
6. **Serve the People (`sources/Serve_the_People.md`)**:
   - Puts community growth and public health impact first.
7. **Theory-Driven Understanding (`sources/Theory_Driven_Understanding.md`)**:
   - Focuses on constructing mathematical and conceptual biological theories instead of simple data accumulation.
8. **Transdisciplinary Collaboration (`sources/Transdisciplinary_Collaboration.md`)**:
   - Bridges the gap between clinicians, engineers, computational scientists, and wet-lab biologists.
9. **Visionary Leadership (`sources/Visionary_Leadership.md`)**:
   - Nurtures future leaders to pioneer bioinformatics tools and methods.
