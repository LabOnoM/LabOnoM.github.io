  • Bioinformatics oriented study

    In this bioinformatics-oriented study, we re-analyzed a publicly available RNA-seq dataset complemented by literature mining. This approach identified mitochondria and the cytoskeleton as the primary targets of O-GlcNAcylation in osteoblasts. By cross-referencing multiple databases (O-GlcNAcAtlas, TFLink, and Ensembl Variation), we constructed a gene regulatory network to identify candidate transcription factors (TFs) involved in O-GlcNAcylation-regulated osteogenesis. Guided by our bioinformatics findings, we assessed morphological changes in mitochondria and the cytoskeleton following Ogt knockout.
